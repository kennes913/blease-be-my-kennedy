Parking:

Downtown Austin parking is notoriously difficult. We recommend saving yourself the stress (so you can enjoy the party!) and either taking a rideshare service from your hotel to and from the reception or parking at the Convention Center (rates are about $10-$15 for 6-7 hours), which is just a couple blocks away. If you're willing to put in the effort, you can always scour the block for street parking; however, there are often three-hour limits on street parking.

Hotels and Lodging:

Since the reception will go until 11PM, we recommend you book a place to stay nearby. Austin has a number of hotels downtown and the surrounding areas, as well as Airbnb options. Below are a few suggestions are Hilton Garden Inn, Hyatt Place, and Courtyard Austin Downtown/Convention Center.
